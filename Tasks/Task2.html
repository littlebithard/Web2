About Max Verstappen

Max Emilian Verstappen (born 30 September 1997)is a Dutch and Belgian racing driver who competes under the Dutch flag in Formula One for Red Bull Racing. Verstappen has won four Formula One World Drivers' Championship titles, which he won consecutively from 2021 to 2024 with Red Bull, and has won 66 Grands Prix across 11 seasons.

Achievements

  • 4 times Formula One World Champion (2021-2024)
  • 66 Grand Prix victories
  • Youngest driver to compete in Formula One (17 years, 166 days)
  • Highest percentage of wins in a season (86.36%)
